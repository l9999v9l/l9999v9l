# Verifiable APAC Initiative Roadmap

## Overview
This document outlines the strategic roadmap for the Verifiable APAC Initiative, spanning from Q1 2026 through Q4 2027. The roadmap highlights key components such as regional hubs, deal origination pipelines, Vs scoring framework, and quarterly milestones.

## Regional Hubs
1. **East Asia Hub**  
   - Locations: Tokyo, Seoul
   - Focus Areas: Technology partnerships, Government collaborations

2. **Southeast Asia Hub**  
   - Locations: Singapore, Jakarta, Manila
   - Focus Areas: Market expansion, Local partnerships

3. **Australia & New Zealand Hub**  
   - Locations: Sydney, Auckland
   - Focus Areas: Innovation, Regulatory compliance

## Deal Origination Pipelines
- **Q1 2026**: Identify and establish relationships with key stakeholders in target regions.  
- **Q2 2026**: Set up a framework for tracking potential deals and collaborations.  
- **Q3 2026**: Initiate first round of deal negotiations.  
- **Q4 2026**: Review and assess pipeline viability.

## Vs Scoring Framework
- **Q1 2026**: Develop scoring criteria for evaluating potential Vs.
- **Q2 2026**: Pilot the Vs scoring model with initial projects.
- **Q3 2026**: Refine scoring model based on pilot feedback.
- **Q4 2026**: Finalize the Vs scoring framework for wider application.

## Quarterly Milestones
- **Q1 2026**: Establish regional teams; complete initial stakeholder mapping.
- **Q2 2026**: Launch pilot programs in regional hubs.
- **Q3 2026**: Secure first partnerships and establish collaborations.
- **Q4 2026**: Full review of the year’s progress and adjustments to the strategy.

- **Q1 2027**: Implement feedback from the previous year; scale successful initiatives.
- **Q2 2027**: Expand outreach efforts and establish additional partnerships.
- **Q3 2027**: Conduct comprehensive evaluations of regional impacts.
- **Q4 2027**: Finalize project outcomes and document lessons learned for future strategies.

## Conclusion
This roadmap serves as a guideline for the strategic direction of the Verifiable APAC Initiative, ensuring alignment with overall business objectives and facilitating growth in the APAC region.
